Trajectories of QST in large-spin representation of 3x3 network.

----------------
| Independents |
----------------
t           : evolution time (ns)

--------------
| Dependents |
--------------                                
sigmas      : dynamics of S_x,y,z, in shapes of [(x,y,z), (1,2), ts()]


-------------
| Constants | 
-------------  
labels      : x,y,z axis of the Bloch sphere

---------- 
| suffix |
----------
_exp        : experimental
_sim        : numerical
_avg        : averaged value
_std        : standard error
